EASY CHEESECAKE RECIPE

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust:

  • Graham Crackers
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Cheesecake Filling:

  • Cream Cheese
  • Sugar
  • Sour Cream
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Prepare the Graham Cracker Crust:
    • Crush graham crackers and mix with melted butter, sugar, and salt.
    • Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to create the crust.
  3. Prepare the Cheesecake Filling:
    • In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth.
    • Add sugar, sour cream, vanilla extract, and eggs. Mix until well combined.
  4. Pour the filling over the crust in the springform pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes or until the center is set.
  6. Allow the cheesecake to cool before refrigerating for a few hours or overnight.
