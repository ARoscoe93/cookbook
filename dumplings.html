EASY DUMPLINGS RECIPE

Ingredients

Dumplings:

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Warm Water
  • Red Cabbage
  • Green Onions
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Soy Sauce

Dipping Sauce:

  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice Wine Vinegar
  • Sesame Oil
  • Red Chili Flake

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and salt. Add warm water gradually, kneading to form a smooth dough.
  2. Roll out the dough and cut into small circles for dumpling wrappers.
  3. Prepare the filling by combining finely chopped red cabbage, green onions, garlic, ginger, and soy sauce.
  4. Place a spoonful of the filling onto each wrapper and fold to seal the dumplings.
  5. Boil or steam the dumplings until cooked.
  6. For the dipping sauce, mix soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, sesame oil, and red chili flake in a small bowl.
  7. Serve the dumplings with the dipping sauce.
